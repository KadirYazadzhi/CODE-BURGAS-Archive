# Sum and Product

## Description
Given a four-digit positive integer, find the sum and product of its digits.

## Input
The first line of the standard input contains a four-digit positive integer.

## Output
On the first line of the standard output, print the sum of the digits of the number.
On the second line, print the product of the digits of the number.

## Example

### Input
```
1234
```

### Output
```
10
24
```

### Explanation
$1+2+3+4=10$
$1 \times 2 \times 3 \times 4=24$
